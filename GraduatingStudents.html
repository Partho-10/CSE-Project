SURVEY QUESTIONNAIRE FOR GRADUATING STUDENTS

Dear Graduating Students,

We kindly request you to complete this survey as part of the graduation process.. Your feedback is crucial for us to improve the academic and student life experience at our institution. Please follow the instructions below to ensure your survey is completed accurately and efficiently.

1. Personal Information:

Fill in your personal information accurately. This includes your name, student ID, department, and program of study. This information helps us categorize feedback appropriately.

2. Survey Questions:

Answer all the questions honestly and thoughtfully. The survey covers various aspects of your academic experience, including:

  • Academic Programs: Quality of courses, curriculum, and teaching methods.
  • Faculty: Accessibility, support, and teaching effectiveness.
  • Facilities: Quality of classrooms, labs, libraries, and other infrastructure.
  • Support Services: Career services, counseling, and administrative support.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Clubs, sports, and other student activities.
  • Overall Experience: Your overall satisfaction with your time at the institution.
